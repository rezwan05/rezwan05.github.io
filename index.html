Sikder Huq

I’m a software engineer specializing in backend infrastructure, distributed systems, and AI platforms. As a founding member of Atlassian’s AI Lab, I build the systems that make AI agents reliable and useful at scale. Previously, I worked on large-scale production systems at Meta, DoorDash, and Amazon. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Iowa, and outside work I pursue independent quantitative research in market data, backtesting, and systematic trading.
